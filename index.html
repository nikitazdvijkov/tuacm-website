Association for Computing Machinery student chapter at the University of Tulsa – the professional organization for compsci majors. We put on frequent social/educational events – plus an annual hackathon – October 13th this semester. Check out our blog. You can get involved by writing content for the blog or by becoming an officer (open positions: advertising, social media, hackathon chair). If you're interested, send us an email: thetuacm@gmail.com. To get notified of TUACM events, sign up for our mailing list.
